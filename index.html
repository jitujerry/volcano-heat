The Legend

Born from
Northeast
Fire

The Bhoot Jolokia — Ghost Pepper — has been grown in the Brahmaputra valley for centuries. Farmers here know it simply as the pepper that makes grown men cry and legends are born. We take only the finest pods at peak heat, slow-cook them into a pickle that carries the full fury of the Northeast.

No shortcuts. No compromises. Just the raw, untamed heat that put Assam on the world spice map.
