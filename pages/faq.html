Frequently Asked Questions

How hot is the Bhoot Jolokia Pickle really?
The Bhoot Jolokia (Ghost Pepper) measures over 1,000,000 Scoville Heat Units — roughly 400 times hotter than a jalapeño. Our pickle preserves the full heat of the pepper. It is genuinely extreme and not recommended for those with low spice tolerance or any digestive conditions.
Are the pickles suitable for vegetarians and vegans?
Yes, all Volcano Heat pickles are 100% vegetarian and vegan. They contain no animal products, dairy, or egg derivatives.
What is the shelf life of the pickle?
Unopened jars have a shelf life of 12 months from the date of production. Once opened, store in a cool, dry place and consume within 3 months for best flavour. Refrigeration after opening is recommended.
Do you ship all over India?
Yes, we ship to all serviceable pin codes across India. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. Remote Northeast locations may take up to 10 days.
Can I order in bulk or wholesale?
Absolutely! We offer wholesale pricing for restaurants, grocery stores, and bulk orders. Please fill out our wholesale enquiry form or email us at wholesale@volcanoheat.in.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), and Net Banking. All transactions are processed securely.
What if my order arrives damaged?
We take great care in packaging, but if your order arrives damaged, please photograph it and email us at hello@volcanoheat.in within 48 hours of delivery. We will send a free replacement within 5–7 business days.
Are your products free of artificial additives?
Yes. We use zero artificial flavours, colours, or preservatives. The lemon juice in our recipe acts as a natural preservative. What you get is pure, real, honest pickle.
