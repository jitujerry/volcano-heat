Privacy Policy

What Data We Collect

In this demo version, all data (account details, orders, reviews) is stored locally in your browser via localStorage/sessionStorage. No data is transmitted to any external server. In a production deployment, we would collect: your name, email, phone, delivery address, and order history — used solely for order fulfilment and communication.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To send order confirmation and tracking updates
  • To notify you of new products (only with your consent)
  • We never sell your data to third parties

Cookies

This site uses only essential session storage for login state. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.
