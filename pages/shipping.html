Policies

Shipping Policy

Delivery Timeline

We ship all orders within 1–2 business days of confirmation. Standard delivery across India takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Remote areas in the Northeast may take up to 10 business days.

Free Shipping

Orders above ₹499 qualify for free standard shipping. Orders below ₹499 attract a flat shipping fee of ₹49.

Courier Partners

We ship via trusted courier partners including Delhivery, Blue Dart, and India Post. You will receive a tracking number via email/SMS once your order is dispatched.

Cash on Delivery

COD is available for orders across most serviceable pin codes in India. COD orders may take 1 additional business day to process. Please ensure someone is available to receive and pay for the package.

Packaging

All orders are carefully packed with bubble wrap and sealed boxes to ensure your pickles arrive safely. We use eco-friendly packaging wherever possible.

Returns & Replacement

We accept returns only for damaged or incorrect products. If your order arrives damaged, please photograph it immediately and contact us at hello@volcanoheat.in within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement.
